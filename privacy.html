Netty Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to your use of Netty, a Netflix browser app for the Apple Vision Pro.

Information We Collect

Netty does not collect any personal information about you directly. However, when you use Netty to access Netflix, Netflix may collect information about your activity on the platform, which may include:

  • The titles you watch
  • Your search history
  • Your viewing preferences
  • Device information, such as your AR headset model

Please refer to Netflix's Privacy Policy for more information about the data they collect and how they use it: https://help.netflix.com/en/account/privacy

Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

Netty itself does not use cookies or other tracking technologies. However, Netflix may use these technologies on their platform, which may be accessed through Netty. Please refer to Netflix's Privacy Policy for more information about their use of cookies and other tracking technologies.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
